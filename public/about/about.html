Scramble That Sample: Which Song Borrowed That Sample?

How to Play

Unleash your musical knowledge and see if you can identify the origins of the samples used in popular songs. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Listen to the Sample

Play the audio clip provided and focus on the distinct sounds, beats, or lyrics that might give away the original song.

2. Guess the Song

Enter your guess in the search bar. If you're unsure, skip for a hint to get closer to the answer!

3. Check Your Answer

Submit your guess and see if you were right! If correct, move on to scramble the next sample and keep playing!

Scramble the Sample

Pro Tips

Focus on unique instrumentals or lyrics in the sample to trace it back to the original song.

Use all hints wisely—they might lead you directly to the answer!

Challenge yourself by guessing without hints for extra fun.

About

SampleScramble is a music guessing game where players listen to a sample and try to guess the popular song that used it. It is a fun challenge for music lovers to test their knowledge of sampling in modern music.
